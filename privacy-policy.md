# Privacy Policy - SleepSupply

**Last Updated:** 03/02/2026

## Overview

SleepSupply ("we", "our", "the app") is a sleep equipment tracking application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Equipment Information**: Names, types, purchase dates of your CPAP/sleep equipment
- **Supply Information**: Supplies, replacement schedules, costs
- **Usage Logs**: Manual entries of equipment usage (if enabled)
- **Custom Categories**: Any categories you create

### Data Collected Automatically
- **HealthKit Data** (Premium, with permission): Sleep hours data from Apple Health
- **Device Information**: For crash reporting and analytics

### Third-Party Data Collection
- **Google AdMob**: Displays ads in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## How We Store Your Data

### Local Storage
All your equipment, supply, and usage data is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's Core Data/SwiftData framework.

### iCloud Sync (If Enabled)
If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data. See [Apple's iCloud Privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### We Do NOT
- Store your data on our servers
- Sell your personal information
- Share your health data with third parties
- Access your data remotely

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:
- Display and manage your equipment and supplies
- Send maintenance and reorder reminders (local notifications)
- Generate usage statistics and reports
- Sync across your devices (if iCloud enabled)

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Google AdMob | Advertising (free version) | Device ID, ad interaction |
| Apple iCloud | Data sync (optional) | All app data (encrypted) |
| Apple HealthKit | Usage tracking (optional) | Read CPAP data |

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Export**: Export your data as PDF or CSV (Premium)
- **Delete**: Delete individual items via swipe or edit buttons
- **Opt-out of Ads**: Purchase Premium to remove ads
- **Opt-out of Tracking**: Deny App Tracking Transparency prompt

## Data Retention

- Data remains on your device until you delete it
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- iCloud data persists until you delete it from iCloud

## Children's Privacy

SleepSupply is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Security

- All data stored locally using iOS encryption
- iCloud data encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- No external servers or databases

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be noted with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.
